Your Rights

Depending on where you live, privacy laws give you specific rights over your personal information. These include rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and similar laws in Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and other jurisdictions.

We honor these rights regardless of where you live, to the extent we reasonably can. If you're not sure which type of request applies, the form below has short descriptions of each — or pick “Other” and describe what you need in your own words.
